Frequently Asked Questions

Here are the frequently asked questions and answers:

Hayao Miyazaki and Isao Takahata are considered the founders and most prominent figures at Studio Ghibli.

"Ghibli" is an Italian word for a hot desert wind, representing the studio's desire to bring fresh ideas to animation.

Ghibli is known for its detailed hand-drawn animation, beautiful landscapes, and often incorporates elements of Japanese folklore and mythology.

Totoro is a giant, cuddly forest spirit considered a symbol of the studio and often associated with childhood wonder and connection to nature.

As of 2023, the most recent Studio Ghibli release is "The Boy and the Heron".
